Sales Research Assistant AI Workflow Automation

Updated 7/27/2025

Every sales meeting that lands on your calendar is a chance to impress, yet the homework that goes into every call can drain a team. Researching each prospect, summarising the findings, choosing the right solution to pitch and then crafting a personal confirmation email and text message usually means jumping between browsers, spreadsheets and inboxes. The Sales Research Assistant AI Workflow Automation pulls all of those steps into a single automated flow. The result is faster research, spotless data entry and high quality outreach that feels handcrafted while freeing your team to focus on revenue generating conversations.

  7. Those assets are appended to the same row in the sheet so reps can send them with a click or trigger an automated send.
