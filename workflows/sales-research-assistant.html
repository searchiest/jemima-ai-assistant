Sales Research Assistant AI Workflow Automation

Business Value: £26,400

Manual prospect research and follow up has always been the hidden time sink inside revenue teams. Reps flip between tabs, hunt for firmographic data, jot notes into spreadsheets then try to craft an email that sounds fresh after their tenth attempt of the day. The Sales Research Assistant AI Workflow turns that grind into a background service. It gathers rich company intelligence, maps the best-fit solutions, writes a personalised email and text then files everything back into your sales database without any extra clicks. The result is a smoother buyer experience and a lighter workload for your team.

What The Workflow Does

The workflow starts with a simple trigger that checks a sheet for newly booked sales calls. Each row contains a name, email, company, web address, business type and the project they want to discuss.

Step 1: The AI research agent reads those inputs and uses a live search tool to compile a concise company overview, current tech stack and the latest public updates. At the same time it queries an internal product catalogue to suggest a primary solution and two secondary fits. A structured parser ensures the output arrives in a clean six-field JSON package.

Step 2: That JSON is written straight back to the master sheet so your CRM and enablement tools always have fresh data.

Step 3: A second agent scans the enriched record, calls a bank of success stories to find the sharpest testimonial match and uses an advanced language model to draft three assets: an engaging subject line, a short personal email and a friendly SMS reminder.

Step 4: Those messages are immediately logged in the same sheet ready for sequencing or manual tweaks.

All of this runs in the background, turning raw booking data into sales-ready intelligence and outreach within a couple of minutes.

Workflow Businesses Benefits

• Software as a Service providers – Automatically surface tech stack and recent updates so reps can position integrations faster. • Marketing agencies – Replace hours of desk research with instant company overviews and testimonial-driven outreach, letting consultants focus on strategy. • E-commerce platforms – Gain real time insight into a retailer’s tools and growth news to craft sharper pitches around conversion and logistics. • B2B manufacturers – Quickly understand a prospect’s operational projects and send proof-laden messages that speak to production efficiency. • Professional services firms – Enrich client records with market context while producing tailored confirmation notes that reinforce expertise. • Recruitment agencies – Pull company initiatives and growth signals to personalise talent proposals at scale. • Fintech providers – Identify existing technology stacks to anchor compliance and payment solutions in the first email. • Managed IT service providers – Detect gaps in a prospect’s stack and reference similar client wins in automated follow ups. • Consulting boutiques – Leverage deep company snapshots to deliver advisory emails that feel handcrafted, not templated. • Event and media companies – Rapidly research sponsors or exhibitors and send on-brand, personable confirmations that boost attendance rates.

Business Value

A skilled sales operations or marketing executive typically spends about: • 20 minutes sourcing a reliable company overview and tech stack • 10 minutes mapping internal solutions to the prospect’s needs • 5 minutes copying findings into the sales sheet • 15 minutes writing a personalised email and SMS Total manual effort per prospect: 50 minutes.

The automated workflow completes the same tasks with virtually no human involvement in roughly 2 minutes of system run time. That saves 48 minutes per prospect.

Average UK salary for a professional who can perform this work is close to £40,000 a year, or roughly £22 per hour. Every prospect processed by the workflow therefore saves about £17.60 in labour cost (0.8 hours × £22).

The Sales Research Assistant AI Workflow shifts prospect research and follow up from a draining chore to an automated asset. Your reps walk into each meeting armed with up-to-date intelligence, while prospects receive a message that feels genuinely personal. The team recovers hundreds of hours, operating costs fall and buyer experience rises.

Adopting the workflow is not just about speed; it de-risks handoffs, enforces data quality and ensures every lead gets the white-glove touch that closes deals. For managers tasked with expanding pipeline without ballooning headcount, it is an immediate performance lever that keeps paying back week after week.
