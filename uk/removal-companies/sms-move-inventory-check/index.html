What happens next

The workflow creates a session, sends the move-inventory SMS, captures the customer reply, records the inventory status or callback request and produces a staff-ready summary.

Demo boundaries

This is a demo. No move date, crew availability, vehicle size, packing service, price, quote, completion time or access arrangement is guaranteed. Do not send payment details, addresses, access codes or sensitive information by SMS.

Why this SMS workflow matters

This SMS demo shows how a removal company can chase move inventory status without relying on repeated manual calls. It helps the team understand whether the customer’s list is ready, whether they need the inventory link again or whether they need a call before planning continues.

  • Removal quotes and move planning can be inaccurate when the inventory is incomplete.
  • Customers may lose the inventory link or delay completing it.
  • Office teams need to know which customers are ready and which need help.
  • SMS should not collect full addresses, payment details, access codes or sensitive move information.
  • The workflow should support planning without confirming price, vehicle size, crew availability or completion time.
