Demo boundaries

Why this SMS workflow matters

This SMS demo shows how a landscaper can keep customers updated when weather or ground conditions affect start timing. It captures whether the customer has acknowledged the update, needs a different arrangement or has a question for the office.

  • Landscaping schedules can shift because of rain, ground conditions or supplier timing.
  • Customers may become frustrated if weather-related updates are unclear.
  • Office teams need to know who has acknowledged an update and who needs a call or response.
  • Start-date changes should be reviewed by staff before any real schedule is changed.
  • The workflow should avoid promises about start dates, completion dates, drainage, design results or project suitability.
