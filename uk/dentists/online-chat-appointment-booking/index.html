Why this workflow matters

Most enquiries a dental practice receives are not clinical questions. They are people trying to book, move or cancel an appointment — work that interrupts reception all day and needed no clinical judgement in the first place.

  • Calls go unanswered at lunch, during treatment, and after the practice closes
  • Reception is interrupted mid-patient to answer the phone
  • Patients who cannot get through book with another practice
  • Voicemails are chased the next morning, by which time the patient has moved on
