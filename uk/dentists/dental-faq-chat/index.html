Why this workflow matters

A large share of dental enquiries are simple information questions — price, availability, NHS status, what a treatment involves — that never needed a clinician, but do need answering fast before the patient books with someone else.

  • Patients leave the site when a price or NHS question is not answered quickly
  • Reception cannot monitor website chat while treating patients
  • Contact-form enquiries sit unanswered for hours and go cold
  • The same handful of questions get asked and re-asked all day
  • Out-of-hours questions get no reply until the next working day

How this could be implemented

The assistant answers only from your own approved information and sits on your existing website. Clinical questions, symptoms, pain and anything urgent are never answered — they are flagged and handed to a human. It captures the patient's details with their consent, and nothing is written to your system that a member of staff cannot see and undo.
