What happens next

The caller speaks naturally. Jemima captures the reason for the call, repeats the message back, stores it, and creates a structured call report showing patient intent, appointment or admin context, callback request, urgency or safety flag, recommended staff action, confidence and whether human review is required.

Demo boundaries

This is a demo. No appointment is booked. No medical advice, diagnosis, treatment guidance, triage, test-result interpretation, referral advice, pricing, availability, emergency or patient-specific advice is provided. Messages are captured for clinic review.

Why this voice workflow matters

This voice demo shows how a private healthcare clinic can capture patient calls safely without using the assistant as a clinical adviser. It is designed to preserve appointment, consultation, referral, results or callback context while flagging any urgent or safety-related language for human review.

  • Patients may call outside office hours with sensitive or clinically relevant concerns.
  • Reception teams need to separate appointment admin from messages that require clinical review.
  • Callers may mention referrals, results, symptoms or treatment enquiries that must not be answered by the AI.
  • Private clinics need fast follow-up without creating unsupported advice or triage.
  • Staff need clear handover notes rather than a vague voicemail.
