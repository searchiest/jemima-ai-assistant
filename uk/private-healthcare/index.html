Why it matters

Where a faster, more consistent clinic wins and keeps patients

Enquiries arrive while reception is with patients

New-patient enquiries come in while your front desk is looking after the people in front of them, and the ones left waiting often book elsewhere.

Valuable appointments lost to no-shows

Private consultation slots are valuable, and unconfirmed appointments lead to gaps that are hard to fill at short notice.

Self-pay and insured questions

Patients repeatedly ask about pricing, insurers, referrals and excesses, and answering each by hand takes time.

Consultation follow-up forgotten

Treatment plans and estimates need following up to convert, but that slips when the front desk is flat out.

Reviews and recalls by hand

Follow-up appointments and review reminders depend on someone remembering, so they’re inconsistent.

Out-of-hours enquiries

Patients enquire in the evenings and at weekends, and those messages sit unanswered until the clinic reopens.
