Turn browsers into buyers with zero late-night campaign fiddling.

Why automate marketing?

Email sequences, social posts, and retargeting ads are proven revenue drivers – but only when they run consistently. Jemima AI installs pre-built campaigns that launch, learn, and optimise while you focus on serving customers.

What the system can handle

Smart Email Drips

Welcomes new leads, nudges inactive ones, and sends offers at the perfect moment.

Social Post Scheduler

Drafts & queues LinkedIn or Facebook posts, then replies to comments with AI prompts.

Ad-Audience Sync

Updates Custom Audiences daily from your CRM so ads follow viewers across channels.

Lead-Scoring Loop

Scores website activity, pushes hot prospects to sales, keeps the rest nurtured.

Micro-Reporting

Sends a one-page highlight email of wins and next actions every Monday – no dashboard trawling.

Evergreen Promo Engine

Runs seasonal or evergreen offers automatically, recycling winners and killing duds.
