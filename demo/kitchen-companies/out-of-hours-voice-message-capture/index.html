Why this voice workflow matters

This voice demo shows how a kitchen company can capture high-intent showroom and project enquiries after hours. It is designed to preserve whether the caller wants a design appointment, quote, worktop replacement, full renovation or callback, without attempting to design or price the kitchen.

  • Kitchen buyers often research in the evening and call outside showroom hours.
  • Designers may be in consultations and unable to answer live calls.
  • Project enquiries need context such as full redesign, replacement units, worktops or appliances.
  • A missed design appointment request can become a lost showroom lead.
  • The business must avoid design or installation advice before reviewing the project.
