Why this SMS workflow matters

This SMS demo shows how an accountant can chase document readiness without asking clients to send sensitive records by text. The workflow is designed to capture status and route the next action, not to collect private documents or provide tax advice.

  • Teams lose time sending repeated reminders for missing records.
  • Clients may not know whether to upload records, ask for the link, or request help.
  • SMS must avoid collecting bank details, ID documents, tax references or login information.
  • Staff need to know whether the client is ready, blocked, or simply missing the upload link.
  • A safe workflow should point clients back to secure channels.

Example SMS conversation

Client texts: 2421
Jemima replies: We are checking whether you have everything ready for your return. Reply READY if your records are prepared, LINK if you need the secure upload link, or SUPPORT if you are unsure what is missing.
Client replies: LINK
Jemima replies: Thanks. Your request for the secure upload link has been captured. Please do not send sensitive documents by SMS.

What the business receives

The workflow helps the accountancy team separate clients who are ready from clients who need a link or human support. It reduces repeated chasing while keeping sensitive document handling outside SMS.
