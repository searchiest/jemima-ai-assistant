AI Customer Service Automation

Resolve routine questions 24/7, keep clients happy, and reclaim your evenings.

Why automate support?

Small businesses live or die by referrals and repeat orders, yet owners rarely have time to answer every “just checking…” email. We deploy & maintain AI agents that clear the queue, handle common issues, and escalate only when a human touch really matters.

What our support bots can do

Inbox Triage

Reads incoming emails, labels urgency, drafts replies, and files tasks to the right person.

Live-Chat Concierge

Pops up on your site, answers FAQs, and books calls when human help is needed.

Ticket Deflection

Pulls help-centre articles and past answers to solve issues before they reach the queue.

Multilingual Replies

Understands and responds in 100+ languages – no outsourcing required.

CRM & Order Sync

Looks up orders, appointments, or policy numbers and includes them in the answer automatically.

Sentiment Alerts

Flags unhappy customers early so you can save the relationship.
